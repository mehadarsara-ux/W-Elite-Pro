About Us

W-Elite Pro is a premium language institute founded by university teachers and language experts. We help professionals, academic scholars, and high-achieving students reach top international standards in English.

Led by Dr. Sara Mehadar (PhD in Comparative Literature) and Senior IELTS Specialist Nissa, our programs focus on academic precision and real-world results. We do not offer casual, informal coaching. Instead, we use structured university-level frameworks to help you succeed. Whether you need to pass the IELTS exam with a high score or present your work on a global stage, W-Elite Pro gives you the skills and confidence to stand out.
